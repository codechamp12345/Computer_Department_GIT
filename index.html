About the Computer Department

The Computer Department at Gharda Institute of Technology (GIT) is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovative research in the field of computer science and engineering. Our department offers undergraduate programs designed to equip students with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills required to excel in the rapidly evolving technology industry.

Key Features:

  • State-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest hardware and software.
  • Experienced faculty members with diverse research interests.
  • Strong industry connections and opportunities for internships.
  • Active student organizations and clubs that promote coding, robotics, and more.
  • Focus on research and development with numerous projects and publications.

Our Vision

To be a leader in computer science education, research, and innovation, contributing to the technological advancement of society.
